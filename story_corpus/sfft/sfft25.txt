<hr>  <h3 align="center">THE BEE AND THE MOUSE.</h3>  
<p>A BEE met a mouse and said--<br> "Come over till we, make a house."<br> "I will not," said Luchag, the mousie.<br> "He to whom thou gavest thy summer honey,<br> Let him make a winter house for thee;<br> I have a little house under the ground, <a name="page_98"><font size="1" color="green">p. 98</font></a><br> That can reach neither cold nor breeze,<br> Thou wilt be a ragged creature,<br> Running on the tops of the trees."</p>
