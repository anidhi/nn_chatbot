<hr>  <h3 align="center">THE CROODIN DOO. <a name="fr_9"></a><a href="#fn_9"><font size="1">1</font></a>  <a name="fr_10"></a><a href="#fn_10"><font size="1">2</font></a>
</h3>  
<p>"WHERE hae ye been a' the day,<br> My bonny wee croodin doo?"<br> "O I hae been at my stepmother's house;<br> Make my bed, mammie, now!<br> Make my bed, mammie, now!"</p>  
<p>"Where did ye get your dinner,<br> My bonny wee croodin doo?"<br> "I got it in my stepmother's;<br> Make my bed, mammie, now, now, now!"<br> Make my bed, mammie, now!"</p>  
<p>"What did she gie ye, to your dinner,<br> My bonny wee croodin doo?"<br> "She ga'e me a little four-footed fish;<br> Make my bed, mammie, now, now, now<br> Make my bed, mammie, now!"</p>  
<p>"Where got she the four-footed fish,<br> My bonny wee croodin doo?"</p>  
<p> </p>  
<p>
</p>  
<p>
</p>  
<p><a name="page_75"><font size="1" color="green">p. 75</font></a></p>  
<p><span class="contnote"><font size="-2" color="GREEN">[paragraph continues]</font></span>  She got it down in yon well strand;<br> O make my bed, mammie, now, now, now!<br> Make my bed, mammie, now!"</p>  
<p>"What did she do wi' the banes o't,<br> My bonny wee croodin doo?"<br> "She ga'e them to the little dog;<br> Make my bed, mammie, now, now, now!<br> Make my bed, mammie, now!"</p>  
<p>"O what became o' the little dog,<br> My bonny wee croodin doo?"<br> "O it shot out its feet and died!<br> O make my bed, mammie, now, now, now<br> O make my bed, mammie, now!"</p>
