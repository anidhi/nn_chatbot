SHROVE TUESDAY AT ST IVES.
FORMERLY it was customary for the boys to tie stones to cords, and with these parade the town, slinging these stones against the doors, shouting aloud,--"Give me a pancake, now--now--now, Or I 'II souse in your door with a row--tow--tow.
